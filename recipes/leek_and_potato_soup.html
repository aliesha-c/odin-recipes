Leek and Potato Soup

Description

A comforting classic, Leek and Potato soup will warm even the coldest of hearts. This recipe is Nagi's from recipetineats, you should visit her page for more details and tips (very useful!).

Ingredients

  • 30g butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • 3 leeks, sliced thinly
  • 1kg potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1.5L chicken/vegetable stock
  • 3/4 cup cream
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • croutons

Steps

  1. In a large pot, over medium heat, melt butter.
  2. Saute garlic and leek until soft and sweet
  3. Add potatoes and broth. Bring to a simmer and cover with a lid.
  4. Gently simmer, covered, for 25min or until potato is very soft.
  5. Take off the heat and puree with a stick blender until just smooth.
  6. Add salt and pepper. Stir through cream.
  7. Served drizzled with cream and sprinkled with chives and croutons
