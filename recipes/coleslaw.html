Coleslaw

Description

A delicous, traditional coleslaw recipe by Nagi from recipetineats. You should visit her page for more details and tips.

Ingredients

  • 7 cups green cabbage, finely shredded
  • 1 medium carrot, shredded
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup sour cream or yoghurt
  • 1 1/2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 3/4 tsp celery salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. To make the dressing, mix everything except for the cabbage and carrots together.
  2. Toss the cabbage, carrots, and dressing made in the previous step all together. Set aside for at least 20min.
  3. Toss well before serving.
